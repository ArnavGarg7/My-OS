# ADR-006 — Context Builder & Budget

- **Status:** Accepted (frozen, Sprint 5.5)
- **Date:** Phase 5.1

## Context

AI prompts need relevant, bounded context assembled from many modules without exceeding token limits or leaking sensitive data.

## Decision

Context is assembled by prioritized **builders** per feature profile; a **Budget Manager** includes builders by priority until the token budget is spent and records what was dropped. Prompts are prefix-stable (no timestamps/interpolation) for cache efficiency. Data classification (`AI_SAFE_SURFACES`) governs what may enter context.

## Consequences

- Context size is observable and enforced; nothing silently overflows.
- Traces record which builders contributed ([ADR-009](ADR-009.md)).
- Sensitive domains stay out of AI context by policy.
